The Persian Meters

after Thackston’s list

The introduction to Wheeler Thackston’s Millennium of Classical Persian Poetry, which contains his primer on Persian prosody, is also available in PDF.

Syllables in “dashes and scoops” should be read left-to-right. The Persian notation is, of course, right-to-left.

Starts with: sh-lo; lo-sh; lo-lo
